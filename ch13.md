survival function s(t) = P(T>t) = 1 - FT(t). where T is the survival time.  FT(t) = P(T<=t);

sj = P(T>tj) = P(T>tj | T>tj-1) p(T>tj-1) = pj * sj

pj = P(T>tj | T>tj-1)

    (0,t1], (t1,t2], (t3,t4], ... , (tj-1, tj] , ..., (tk-1, tk]
    
    rj = # at risk in jth interval
    dj = # died of tj
    cj = # right-censored at tj
